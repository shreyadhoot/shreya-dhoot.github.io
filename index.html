About

I am a Software Engineer with about eight years of experience of designing and developing processes, applications and APIs along with exposure to DevOps and Database.


My interest lies in user-centered design, coding with a knack of problem-solving, leading cross-functional teams and creating exceptional user experiences. I have successfully led projects from concept to launch, keeping a track of good user satisfaction and driving business growth.


My journey started as an Integration developer where I learned and worked on creating SSIS packages, jobs and BizTalk interfaces. Over two years I transitioned into Backend development and later into Fullstack development. I am originally from Indore and did my Bachelor of Engineering degree in Computer Science there. Post college, I moved to Bangalore for my first job at Citrix R&D, India.
